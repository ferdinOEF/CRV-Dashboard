Goa Climate Risks

Coastal Erosion: High (Candolim, Colva)
Cyclone Vulnerability: Medium–High. Stronger cyclones (Fani, Tauktae) increasing.
Sea-Level Rise: 14% land area at risk by 2050
Heat Stress: More >35°C days, esp. Quepem, Sanguem
Deforestation: Western Ghats threatened by mining & roads
Urban Flooding: Rising incidents (last 5 yrs)
