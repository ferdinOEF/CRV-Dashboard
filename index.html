Goa Climate Risks

🌊 Coastal & Marine
• Coastal erosion (Candolim, Colva, Betul)
• Sea-level rise → 14% land risk by 2050
• Cyclones → Increasing intensity

🌡️ Climate & Health
• Heat stress (>35°C days in Quepem, Sanguem)
• Malaria/Dengue linked to flooding

🌳 Forests & Biodiversity
• Mining/roads fragment Western Ghats
• Wetland & mangrove pressure

🌆 Urban & Infrastructure
• Urban flooding (Panaji, Margao, Mapusa)
• Tourism: erosion + water scarcity
